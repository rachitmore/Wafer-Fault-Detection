Welcome to My Journey in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Rachit More, a passionate Data Science and Artificial Intelligence enthusiast with a unique background in Mechanical Engineering and Quality Control. My journey into the world of AI began during my Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering, where an IT convention and a short-term training program on autonomous vehicles ignited my interest in AI.

My Journey

Mechanical Engineering Foundations

During my undergraduate studies, I developed a strong foundation in mathematics and programming, which later proved invaluable in my AI pursuits.

Quality Control Experience

As a Quality Control Incharge, I honed my skills in implementing and maintaining quality control procedures, monitoring production processes, and leading a team. I ensured vendor materials met the required specifications and documented inspections and tests for compliance during audits.

Transition to AI and Data Science

My passion for AI led me to explore data science through various online resources. This profound interest compelled me to leave my job and pursue a full-time career in data science. As a Data Science Intern, I gathered and preprocessed data, conducted exploratory data analysis, developed and tested machine learning models, and contributed to open-source projects.

Current Role

I currently work as a Technical Support Engineer at Meta Programming Technologies. In this role, I troubleshoot and resolve client issues, monitor software performance, assist in testing strategies, create technical documentation, and collaborate closely with developers to communicate client feedback effectively.

My Goals

My goal is to continue expanding my expertise in Data Science and AI, contributing to innovative projects that make a difference. I am particularly interested in areas such as deep learning, computer vision, and AI ethics.

Let's Connect

I am always excited to connect with fellow enthusiasts, professionals, and mentors in the Data Science and AI community. Feel free to reach out to me.
